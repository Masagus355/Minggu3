Group-B Rallye Tribute

The following video is a tribute to the rallying in the middle 80's
whence the Group B monster machines dominated the championship.
The tracks were extremely dangerous and the safety barriers were made out of the crowds.
Since the cars became as much as powerful as the F1 cars at the time,
it was too much for the human body to keep the monsters in the tracks.
And so, in 1986, after several horrible crashes,
The FIA banned Group B rally forever.
